The year is 2339 CE. Humans have been extinct for over 200 years due to a nuclear holocaust

As the dust settles down in this post-apocalyptic world, the only remaining life-forms are the living machines Limae the activated Plants-ActiFlorae.

Post the nuclear holocaust, the Limae have managed to gain a stronghold on the only planet supporting life in this solar system.
ActiFlorae have slowly evolved over the past 200 yearsand now ready to fight back.
Both ActiFlorae and Limae have consistently competed for the most valuable remaining resource on Earth clean water (WATR).



Limae need the Watr to ensure their systems remain cool. Plants survive on Watr and want it to eventually nurture other beings that would appear back on the One Earth.
